Are the Dallas Cowboys ever going to win another Super Bowl and secure a sixtth ring?

2022-04-29

Are the Dallas Cowboys content with their 5 rings or will they take advantage of this off season and have the chance to compete in Super Bowl LVII? The Dallas Cowboys stand amongst other top ranked teams regarding Super Bowl rings, falling just 1 ring short from the Patriots and Steelers, both teams having 6. Can they tie for first and secure another Super Bowl ring this upcoming season?

The Dallas Cowboys have not won a Super Bowl this century … That does not sound right??

Have they been playing just as well in recent seasons as they were back when they were winning Super Bowls?? The DraftKings Sportsbook states the Dallas Cowboys have +1400 odds to win the 2023 Super Bowl. How can that be possible?

In recent years, the Cowboys have been good but never great… So let’s take a look at a few different areas in the seasons to see if they stand a chance?
